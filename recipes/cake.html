A cake is a baked, sweet dessert typically made from flour, sugar, and other ingredients like eggs, butter, and baking powder. It can be simple or elaborate, and often includes frosting or decorations. Cakes can be found in various shapes and sizes, from individual cupcakes to multi-layered birthday cakes
